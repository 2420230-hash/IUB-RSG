About Us

The IUB Rover Scout Group is a recognized unit under Bangladesh Scouts, operating at Independent University, Bangladesh. The group focuses on leadership development, discipline, community service, and character building among students through scouting activities.

Mission & Activities

The group actively participates in social welfare programs, leadership training, volunteer services, national events, disaster response, and various scouting-based skill development activities while upholding the principles of Bangladesh Scouts.
